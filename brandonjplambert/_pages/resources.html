My Learning Strategy

Multi-Modal Engagement Approach

Through years of language learning, I've developed a comprehensive strategy that combines multiple approaches:

  • Visual Learning: Using Unsplash and personal photos for vocabulary building and scene description practice
  • Geographic Immersion: Virtual exploration through Geoguessr and Google Earth for contextual learning
  • Systematic Retention: Converting messy notes into structured Anki flashcards for spaced repetition
  • AI Enhancement: Leveraging LLMs for note transformation, exercise generation, and interactive practice
  • Social Integration: Following authentic Spanish accounts for real-world language exposure
  • Active Production: Moving from passive consumption to active narration and description

The key is using personal life experiences as learning material - this emotional resonance significantly improves retention and engagement.
