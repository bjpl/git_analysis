# Sample Questions for Questionnaire System

1. What is your name?

2. How would you describe your current mood in three words?

3. What is the most interesting thing you learned this week?

4. If you could have dinner with any historical figure, who would it be and why?

5. Describe a challenge you overcame recently and what you learned from it.

6. What book, movie, or TV show has had the biggest impact on your thinking?

7. If you could change one thing about the world, what would it be?

8. What skill would you most like to develop or improve?

9. Describe your ideal weekend activity.

10. What advice would you give to someone just starting their career?

11. How do you define success in your personal life?

12. What role does technology play in your daily routine?

13. If you could travel anywhere in the world, where would you go and what would you want to experience there?

14. What is something you believe that most people disagree with?

15. How do you handle stress or difficult situations?
